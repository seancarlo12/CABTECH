## 📖 Overview

The **CabTech Auto Services Scheduling and Tracking System** is a web-based platform developed as a capstone project for **CabTech Auto Services**, an automotive repair and maintenance shop.

The system was designed to replace manual scheduling, service tracking, and record management processes with a centralized digital solution. It allows customers, staff, and administrators to efficiently manage automotive service operations through an integrated web application.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Traditional service management processes relied heavily on paper records and manual coordination, leading to:

- Scheduling conflicts and appointment overlaps
- Difficulty tracking service progress
- Delayed customer communication
- Inconsistent record management
- Time-consuming report generation
- Limited operational visibility

---

## 💡 Solution

Developed a centralized web-based system that enables:

- Online appointment scheduling
- Service request management
- Service progress tracking
- Customer and vehicle record management
- Administrative reporting
- Automated email notifications

The system improves operational efficiency and provides better visibility into service activities.
